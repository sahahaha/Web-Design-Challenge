Latitude vs. Max Temperature



Observations


The process selected over 500 cities. As you can see on the graph to the left, temperatures dropped rapidly the farther north travelled from the equator. For example, temperature drops below 50 degrees Fahrenheit at about 70 parallel north. When travelling south past the equator, temperatures drop but not nearly as much. This could be because the dataset did not include cities that far below the equator.
